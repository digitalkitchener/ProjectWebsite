What we did:
Created a progressive web app with the goal of digitizing the Little Black Book.

Why we did it:
To allow youths to access resources online and save them for offline access.

What tech we used:
- Figma for design
- Amplify Studio (Frontend, backend, and deployment)
- Framework: React.js

What we learned:
Amplify Studio is powerful for developing apps however requires extensive knowledge of framework being used.
